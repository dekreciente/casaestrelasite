Molelos


Casa Estrela is situated in the village of Molelos (google maps link), nestled in the district of Viseu, within the Beira Alta province in Portugal’s Centro region. Molelos spans 14.83 km² and is home to 2,225 residents, according to the 2021 census.


We are 159km from Porto and 269km from Lisbon if you come by plane to any of those cities.
